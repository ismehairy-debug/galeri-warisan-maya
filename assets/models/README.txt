KERIS 3D MODEL
==============

keris/ holds the glTF export of "Keris" by apasipepgapuak on Sketchfab
(CC Attribution -- see keris/license.txt):
https://sketchfab.com/3d-models/keris-e66c540f891b426689dbd45fabb1f0a1

The KERIS & ANYAMAN station loads assets/models/keris/scene.gltf at
startup, replacing the flat keris photo; if the load ever fails the
photo plaque comes back automatically. The CC Attribution credit line
is appended to the station's description when the model loads.
